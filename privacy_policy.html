Privacy Policy for Lithox Epoxy App

Last updated: October 11, 2025

1. Introduction

Lithox Epoxy ("we," "our," or "us") operates the Lithox Epoxy mobile application (the "App"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our App.

By using the App, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Personal Information

We collect the following personal information that you provide to us:

  • Name: To identify you and personalize your experience
  • Email Address: To communicate with you about your service requests
  • Phone Number: To contact you regarding consultations and orders
  • Address: To provide location-based services and schedule consultations

2.2 Photos and Images

When you book a consultation, you may upload photos of your space. We use the camera permission to allow you to:

  • Take photos directly from the app
  • Upload existing photos from your device gallery
  • Provide us with visual information about your flooring project

Camera Permission: We request camera access only when you choose to take photos. You can deny this permission and still use the app by uploading existing photos.

2.3 Service Request Information

  • Project details and specifications
  • Service type preferences
  • Project area measurements
  • Order history and status

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

  • Service Delivery: To process your consultation requests and provide epoxy flooring services
  • Communication: To contact you regarding your orders, appointments, and project updates
  • Project Assessment: To evaluate your uploaded photos and provide accurate quotes
  • Order Management: To track and manage your service orders
  • Customer Support: To respond to your inquiries and provide assistance
  • App Improvement: To improve our app functionality and user experience

4. Data Storage and Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your personal information:

  • Secure Storage: Your data is stored securely on our servers
  • Encryption: Sensitive data is encrypted during transmission
  • Local Storage: Some data is stored locally on your device for offline access
  • Access Control: Only authorized personnel have access to your personal information

5. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. We may share your information only in the following circumstances:

  • Service Delivery: With our team members who need the information to complete your service
  • Legal Requirements: When required by law or to protect our rights
  • Business Transfer: In the event of a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets

We do NOT share your information with advertisers or marketing companies.

6. Your Rights and Choices

You have the following rights regarding your personal information:

  • Access: Request access to your personal data
  • Correction: Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your personal data
  • Withdrawal: Withdraw consent for data processing at any time
  • Camera Permission: You can revoke camera access in your device settings at any time

To exercise these rights, please contact us at the email address provided below.

7. Permissions Used

The app requests the following permissions:

  • Camera: To take photos of your space for consultation purposes (optional)
  • Storage/Photos: To select and upload existing photos from your device (optional)
  • Internet: To send and receive data for order processing and communication
  • Phone: To enable you to call us directly from the app (optional)

You can control these permissions in your device settings. Denying permissions may limit some app functionality but will not prevent you from using the app.

8. Children's Privacy

Our App is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have collected information from a child under 13, please contact us immediately.

9. Third-Party Services

Our App may contain links to third-party services (such as phone dialer, email client, or WhatsApp). These third-party services have their own privacy policies. We are not responsible for their privacy practices.

10. Data Retention

We retain your personal information for as long as necessary to:

  • Provide our services to you
  • Comply with legal obligations
  • Resolve disputes and enforce our agreements

You can request deletion of your account and data at any time by contacting us.

11. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by:

  • Posting the new Privacy Policy in the App
  • Updating the "Last updated" date at the top of this policy
  • Sending you a notification through the App (for significant changes)

You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes.

12. Advertising and Analytics

We do NOT use advertising IDs or display ads in our app. We do not track you for advertising purposes or share your data with ad networks.
